My favorite hobby

Cars

In my opinion a car enthusiast is someone who see's vehicles as a sort of living breathing thing.
To me someone who can see that cars not only have a heart and soul but are very alive makes them a car enthusiast.
If you see vehicles as more than just transportation but as an item with a soul that given it being the right car connects with you on a deeper level than just the driving portion.
If you can see the heart of a car you're an enthusiast to me.

It all started when i was about 15-16 years old, some of my friends took me out to some late night touge runs

Now that i'm older my hobby has went more and more for the track-direction with Time-Attack as the main interest

Touge

Illegal and now turned professional form of racing in Japan involving two cars chasing each other single file through a twisty course, traditionally mountain roads.
If the chase car catches up by the end it wins, if the lead car pulls away it wins.
Popularized in the US by the recent "JDM craze" and JDM option videos

Time Attack

A time attack is a type of motorsport where the race cars compete against a clock, and not directly against each other.
Each car is timed through one circuit of the track, and there are no other cars on the track at that time.
The car makes a preliminary circuit, then runs the timed lap, and then finishes with a cool-down lap.
